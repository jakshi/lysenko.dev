Interesting idea - put in your project CLAUDE.md:

> "If you encounter something surprising in this project, alert me and suggest adding it to this file."

And that's it - nothing more.

CLAUDE.md goes to every prompt. Affects every prompt.

And I was reading another one article on how adding too much to your AI agent context window kills your AI agent performance: [Your CLAUDE.md Is Making Your Agent Dumber](https://medium.com/@cdcore/your-claude-md-is-making-your-agent-dumber-953f6dbed308)

Best advice I saw before recommended to keep it under 50-100-200 lines, write it yourself, and put there only most essential information.

The article suggests using that one-liner to find out what IS essential for your agent.

Worth a try.
