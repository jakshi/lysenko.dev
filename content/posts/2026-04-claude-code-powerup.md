After shipping `/buddy` — a Tamagotchi-style terminal pet that lives next to your prompt — Anthropic continues with gamification. `/powerup` adds several interactive lessons with animated demos, right inside the terminal.

![Claude Code /powerup screenshot](/img/claude-code-powerup.jpg)

1. **Talk to your codebase** — files, line refs
2. **Steer with modes** — shift+tab, plan, auto
3. **Undo anything** — /rewind, fix-it-sc, esc
4. **Run in the background** — tasks, /tasks
5. **Teach Claude your rules** — CLAUDE.md, /memory
6. **Extend with tools** — MCP, /mcp
7. **Automate your workflow** — skills, hooks
8. **Multiply yourself** — subagent, /agents
9. **Code from anywhere** — remote-control, /teleroam
10. **Dial the model** — model, /effort

I hope Anthropic ships more of these — seems fun. For more serious learning there is [Anthropic Academy](https://www.anthropic.com/learn).
